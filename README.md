#**Product & Region wise Trends and insights.**

**The main objective of this project is to:**

Monitor overall sales performance
Compare region-wise revenue and growth
Identify top-performing and underperforming regions
Track KPIs such as Total Sales, Profit, Quantity Sold, and Growth %
Enable management to make strategic business decisions

**Key Features & Visuals**

**•	KPI Cards**
o	Total Sales
o	Total Profit
o	Total Orders / Quantity
o	Profit Margin %

**•	Region-wise Analysis**
o	Sales distribution by region (Bar/Column Chart)
o	Profit comparison across regions
o	Region contribution percentage

**•	Trend Analysis**

o	Monthly/Yearly Sales Trend (Line Chart)
o	YoY growth comparison

**•	Category/Product Analysis**

o	Sales by Product Category
o	Top 5 / Bottom 5 Products

**•	Interactive Filters (Slicers)**

o	Region
o	Date (Year / Month)
o	Product Category

**•	Drill-through & Tooltips**
o	Detailed region-level insights
o	Product-level breakdown
________________________________________
**🔹 Data Preparation**
•	Cleaned and transformed raw sales data using Power Query Editor
•	Created relationships between tables (Fact & Dimension tables)
•	Built calculated columns and measures using DAX
o	Total Sales = SUM(Sales Amount)
o	Total Profit = SUM(Profit)
o	Profit Margin % = DIVIDE(Total Profit, Total Sales)
o	Year-over-Year Growth %
